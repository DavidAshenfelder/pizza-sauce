"Let the sauce bring you back to 1895"

It all started in 1895 when Giovanni Dominick Porzio I stowed away on a ship in Naples, Italy at the age of 14 and traveled to the States where he pioneered the first pizzeria in Savannah, Georgia. Porzio’s Restaurant was a local favorite that featured authentic Italian dishes and Neapolitan style pizzas. The restaurant was passed down through several generations of Porzio’s where each John D. Porzio contributed their own skills and passion to family operated business. The restaurant remained in the family until its close in <1969 class=""> To this day, many patrons of the restaurant still recall their experiences at Porzio’s and the rich flavors of the Porzio Italian sauce. Now, the great great-granddaughter is bringing back the joyful experience of the restaurant through the famous pizza sauce that will once again be shared with the community.
